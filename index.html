Kabuga Presbyterian School

Harmonizing Faith, Wisdom, and the Natural World

Welcome to Kabuga Presbyterian School, a sanctuary of education inspired by faith and nature's wonder. Rooted in the rich traditions of Presbyterian beliefs, our school instills moral strength and spiritual growth in each student. We celebrate the bounties of nature, incorporating outdoor learning and environmental stewardship into our holistic education approach. Our aim is to equip students with the tools to think critically, act ethically, and contribute positively to the world.
